About us

Mixing Louisiana-inspired specialties with world-famous Filipino hospitality, Catch 'N Hatch serves hatched-from-scratch comfort dishes with deliciously catchy flavors-think fresh stir fried seafood and buttermilk fried chicken with your choice of classic or trendy flavors-alongside other tasty creations in a lively and familiar dining atmosphere.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the Philippines' household brand for flavored seafood and fried chicken in the next decade.

Our Mission

Our mission is to use our tried-and-tested restaurant experience to consistently serve enjoyable specialty comfort dishes in a fun and friendly casual dining atmosphere.
